  • Medical bio data form

IME Investigation List

United State of America

  1. Physical Examination - Required for all applicants, regardless of age
  2. Interferon Gamma Release Assay (IGRA) Test - Required for applicants 2-14 years of age
  3. Chest X-ray (CXR) - Required for all adult applicants aged 15 and above, or children who had exposure to TB Cases or history of TB disease
  4. Syphilis Test - Required for applicants aged 15 to 44 years of age
  5. Gonorrhea Test - Required for applicants aged 18 to 24 years of age
  6. COVID-19 Screening and Vaccination
